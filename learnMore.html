About this project

The Kenyon College Gund Gallery is a beautiful space filled with a wide range of art that can inspire various emotions in its visitors. With its well curated exhibitions and diverse collection, the gallery is an important cultutal destinaton in Ohio. This website has been designed to give visitors a platform to share their thoughts and feelings about the art on display. This website is an excellent way for visitors to engage with the artwork personally. In the past, vistors to the Gund Gallery would often move through the space silently, taking in the art without much interatction. However, this website will encourage visitors to share their thoughts and feelingd sbout the art they see. This creates a more interactive experience for visitors and provides valuable feedback for the gallery.This feedback can help the curators make more informed decisions about future exhibitions, and it can also help them better understand the impact that the art is having on visitors.
