Frequently Asked Questions

Just click the "sign up" button at the top of the homepage or in the navigation bar. Enter your information and press submit. You will now be able to log in with the username and password you created!

Navigate to the art work page by pressing the "Art Work" button in the navigation bar. Find the artwork you wish to react to and press "react". You will be directed to a response section, where you can select the emotion that best corresponds with your feelings associated with that artwork. Press submit, and your response will be recorded. Note: You will need to sign up or log in before submitting a response.

All the artwork for this project is housed at the Gund Gallery here at Kenyon College. Stop by the gallery to see any of these pieces, and be sure to use this website to submit your reactions!
